Welcome to Tinder Bot!

Tinder Bot is a powerful tool designed to automate various actions on Tinder, including swiping, messaging, and profile management. Whether you're looking to save time or increase your match potential, this bot can help you optimize your Tinder experience by automating tasks that would otherwise take up valuable time.

Features of Tinder Bot

1. Smart Swiping

Automate right/left swipes based on preferences such as age, distance, interests, and more.

2. Instant Messaging

Send personalized messages to new matches automatically, so you never miss an opportunity to connect.

3. Profile Management

Keep your profile fresh and up-to-date by automatically updating your photos, bio, and interests.

4. Scheduled Actions

Schedule swipes and messages during peak times for maximum engagement with potential matches.

5. Proxy & Fingerprint Handling

Protect your anonymity while automating your actions with proxy support and advanced fingerprint handling techniques.

Installation Guide

Follow the steps below to get started with Tinder Bot:

  1. Download the latest release from GitHub Releases.
  2. Enable installations from unknown sources on your Android device (Settings > Security > Install from unknown sources).
  3. Install the APK file and open the app.
  4. Configure your preferences, such as age, distance, and messaging templates.
  5. Start automating your Tinder experience!

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Tinder Bot safe to use?

Yes, Tinder Bot follows best practices to ensure your account remains safe while automating tasks. We recommend using it responsibly and not overusing automation to avoid detection.

2. Can I customize the reply templates?

Absolutely! You can personalize messages with placeholders like {first_name} and {interest} to make them feel more human.

3. How do schedules work?

You can schedule swipes and messages to run at specific times to increase engagement during peak hours.

4. Does it use a lot of battery/data?

The bot is designed to be efficient and minimizes battery and data usage by only running necessary tasks at optimal times.
